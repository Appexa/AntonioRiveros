About me

My introduction

Antonio Riveros, CEO and founder of Drontec, Creotec, and Creo, is a serial entrepreneur and passionate electromechanical engineer recognized for his innovative DNA. He has received notable awards, such as Best Startup in Bolivia at Seedstars 2020, and first place in the "Nada Te Detiene" program in 2020. During the pandemic, he manufactured emergency respirators for hospitals in Bolivia. With his commitment to entrepreneurial development and social impact, Antonio believes in the power of science, research, innovation, and entrepreneurship to strengthen the economy. His passion for generating significant change is driven by his family and his two children.
